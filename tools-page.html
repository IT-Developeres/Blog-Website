Friendship, Love Calculator

Our Love and Friendship Calculator is designed to bring a touch of fun, curiosity, and positivity into relationships. Using simple name-based compatibility logic, it provides an entertaining way to explore the bond between two people — whether it’s love, friendship, or both!

While it’s not based on complex AI algorithms or scientific matchmaking, the calculator is a lighthearted tool to celebrate connections and share smiles. Just enter two names, and let the magic reveal your compatibility score — because sometimes, love and friendship are best enjoyed with a bit of playful technology.
